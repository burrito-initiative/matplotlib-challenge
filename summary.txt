SUMMARY Pymaceuticals: 
============ 

Tumor Response to Treatment: Assuming tumor growth continues to increase without treatment, or without effective treatment, Capomulin is the only observed drug to effectively decrease tumor size. 

Metastatic Response to Treatment: While all observed drug treatments saw an increase of number Metastatic Sites, Capomulin proved to resulted in the leaset amount of sites. 

Survival Rates: Capomulin, as expected from previous observations, resulted with the highest survival rate.
